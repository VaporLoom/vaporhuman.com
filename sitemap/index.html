This site map is a normal HTML page. The crawler-oriented version lives at sitemap.xml, and crawler policy lives at robots.txt.

VaporHuman designs private AI continuity systems. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the product lane develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or professional advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

The work-intake path uses a hosted Tally form, and paid discovery uses a hosted Stripe Payment Link. This site does not embed forms, login, checkout, public email address, donation flow, subscriptions, or live assistant service.
