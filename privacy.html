Privacy

Plain data boundaries for a hosted-provider site.

VaporHuman keeps the public site static and uses hosted providers for intake, payment, and shop checkout.

This page explains what is active now, what is not active, and which providers handle the live workflows.

Current date

Effective May 21, 2026.

This policy applies to vaporhuman.com and the linked hosted-provider workflows used for work intake, paid discovery, and the creator shop. It is a practical operating notice, not legal advice.

What is collected

The live paths are hosted by providers.

Work intake

Tally hosts the work-intake form. The form may ask for company/project details, contact details, service interest, buying stage, goals, message, and consent.

Paid discovery

Stripe hosts the paid-discovery checkout. Stripe processes payment details; VaporHuman does not receive or store full card data in this site.

Creator shop

Fourthwall hosts the merch and creator-commerce shop. Orders, memberships, shipping, and storefront checkout are handled through Fourthwall.

Static site

This build includes an optional Google Analytics tag that loads only after a visitor allows analytics. Declining analytics keeps the tag unloaded.

Site measurement

Google Analytics may report aggregate page visits and user-attribute reports when available. VaporHuman uses this for internal admin review, not member-facing decisions.

How information is used

Use stays tied to the request.

Respond

Use submitted contact and request details to review the request, reply, schedule discovery, or decide whether a larger scoped project makes sense.

Deliver

Use payment and service context to deliver paid discovery, keep basic business records, send receipts through providers, and support completed work.

Protect

Use records to prevent abuse, investigate provider/payment issues, maintain site reliability, and comply with applicable business obligations.

Provider links

Review the provider policies before using hosted flows.

Paid discovery

Stripe

Stripe hosts the paid-discovery checkout. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Optional analytics

Google Analytics

Google Analytics loads only after a visitor allows analytics. You can reset your analytics choice on this page.

Analytics

Analytics is optional and consent-gated.

VaporHuman uses Google Analytics for aggregate site measurement only after a visitor allows analytics. The site does not load the Google tag before that choice.

Demographic and interest reports, if enabled in Google Analytics, are aggregate reports and may be unavailable when Google applies privacy thresholds. They are for admin-only trend review, not account eligibility or member-facing personalization.

Do not submit

Keep sensitive material out of public intake.

Do not submit passwords, recovery codes, private keys, payment card numbers, bank details, identity documents, protected health information, emergency requests, or sensitive personal data through the public intake form.

If a project later requires private documents or account access, that should happen only after a separate scoped agreement and a safer transfer method are approved.

Retention and requests

Records are kept only as long as needed for the work.

VaporHuman may keep intake, payment, service, and support records for as long as needed to respond, deliver service, maintain business records, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, or meet provider/legal obligations.

Until a public support email is established, use the VaporHuman Work Intake form for privacy or support requests and include only the minimum context needed.

Children and updates

This site is for adult business use.

VaporHuman is not directed to children. This policy may change as provider flows, support channels, analytics, accounts, subscriptions, or dashboard features are added through separate approval gates.

VaporHuman designs private AI continuity systems. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the product lane develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or professional advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

The work-intake path uses a hosted Tally form, and paid discovery uses a hosted Stripe Payment Link. This site does not embed forms, login, checkout, public email address, donation flow, subscriptions, or live assistant service. Optional Google Analytics measurement loads only after a visitor allows analytics.
