Private information

Do not send sensitive details through public paths.

Do not submit passwords, recovery codes, private keys, payment card numbers, bank details, identity documents, protected health information, emergency requests, or sensitive personal data through public intake or public support paths.

If private files, account access, or regulated work becomes necessary, it should wait for a separate written scope, approved transfer method, and support boundary.

Support boundaries

Policies and support routing are public.

Review Privacy, Terms, and Refund and Support before sending additional context or paying for discovery.

This page is only a confirmation and routing surface. It does not prove receipt, guarantee a response time, create a subscription, or activate a larger project.

VaporHuman designs private AI continuity systems. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the product lane develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or regulated advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

The work-intake path uses a hosted Tally form, and paid discovery uses a hosted Stripe Payment Link. This site does not embed forms, login, checkout, public email address, public support flow, subscriptions, or live assistant service. This build does not include analytics or tracking scripts.
