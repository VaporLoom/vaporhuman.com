Provider gates

Before leads or money move.

  1. Choose lead destination: CRM, email provider, database, or private queue.
  2. Choose checkout provider and product catalog owner.
  3. Approve privacy notice, retention, refunds, receipts, taxes, and support policy.
  4. Add spam protection and failure-state copy before public lead capture.
  5. Verify provider settings and rollback before deployment.
