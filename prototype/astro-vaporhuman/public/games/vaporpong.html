VaporPong

An early static browser-game prototype. It runs locally in this page and does not require login, checkout, tracking, or a live assistant.

Default: CPU Player 1 | Player 2: Arrow Up/Down | 2 Player mode: W/S

Ready.

In CPU mode, pointer and touch controls move Player 2. In 2 Player mode, they move the paddle on the side where contact begins.
