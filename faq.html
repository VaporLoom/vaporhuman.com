FAQ

Short answers for the current site.

What is VaporHuman?

A trust and continuity surface for explaining human-centered assistant systems.

Is this a product launch?

No. This is an early static site, not a service availability announcement.

Can I create an account?

No. There is no login, dashboard, account system, or live assistant service here.

Can I contact VaporHuman?

Not through this site yet. No public email address or contact form is active.

Are donations open?

No. Donations, payments, fundraising, and checkout flows are not active.

Does this provide professional advice?

No. It is not medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, or emergency advice.

Still true

The human remains responsible for decisions.

VaporHuman can describe safer assistant patterns, but it does not make AI output automatically true, safe, complete, private, or appropriate for every context.

VaporHuman is an early static trust surface. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the project develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or professional advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

No forms, login, checkout, payment flow, analytics, tracking scripts, contact capture, public email address, donation flow, or live assistant service are active here.
