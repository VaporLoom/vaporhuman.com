FAQ

Short answers for the current site.

What is VaporHuman?

A trust and continuity surface for explaining human-centered assistant systems.

Is this a product launch?

No. This is an early site, not a service availability announcement.

Can I create an account?

No. There is no login, dashboard, account system, or live assistant service here.

Can I contact VaporHuman?

Yes, for work requests through the hosted Tally intake link on the Work page. No public email address is active.

Are donations open?

No. Donations and fundraising are not active. A Stripe-hosted paid discovery session is available for qualified work requests.

Does this provide professional advice?

No. It is not medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, or emergency advice.

Still true

The human remains responsible for decisions.

VaporHuman can describe safer assistant patterns, but it does not make AI output automatically true, safe, complete, private, or appropriate for every context.

VaporHuman designs private AI continuity systems. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the product lane develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or professional advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

The work-intake path uses a hosted Tally form, and paid discovery uses a hosted Stripe Payment Link. This site does not embed forms, login, checkout, public email address, donation flow, subscriptions, or live assistant service. Optional Google Analytics measurement loads only after a visitor allows analytics.
