FAQ

Open the answer you need.

Site What is VaporHuman?

A public trust surface for private AI continuity systems, human approval gates, memory boundaries, and assistant handoffs.

Site Is this a full product launch?

No. This is an early static site with clear provider-hosted routes and restrained public claims.

Accounts Can I create an account?

No. There is no login, dashboard, account system, or live assistant service here.

Work Can I contact VaporHuman?

Yes, for work requests through the hosted Tally intake link on the Work page. No public email address is active.

Payments How does paid discovery work?

The paid discovery route opens a Stripe-hosted payment page. The site does not embed checkout or process card data.

Trust Does this provide regulated advice?

No. It is not medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, or emergency advice.

Privacy Does the site track visitors?

This build does not include analytics scripts, tracking pixels, hidden forms, cookies, or social embeds.

Shop Where does the shop go?

The shop link opens Fourthwall externally. Fourthwall handles storefront checkout and shop support.

Still true

The human remains responsible for decisions.

VaporHuman can describe safer assistant patterns, but it does not make AI output automatically true, safe, complete, private, or appropriate for every context.

VaporHuman designs private AI continuity systems. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the product lane develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or regulated advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

The work-intake path uses a hosted Tally form, and paid discovery uses a hosted Stripe Payment Link. This site does not embed forms, login, checkout, public email address, public support flow, subscriptions, or live assistant service. This build does not include analytics or tracking scripts.
