Decision boundary

Choose the work state and see the next boundary.

Scattered context Build continuity before more output.

Assistant may draft: source maps, handoff notes, summaries, and next-step options.

Human approves: what becomes durable memory, what leaves the workspace, and what stays parked.

Stays private: secrets, raw sessions, provider screens, payment data, private messages, and unreviewed identity details.

Public claim Separate draft language from publication.

Assistant may draft: restrained copy, claim checks, evidence gaps, and launch checklists.

Human approves: final wording, publication, provider changes, and rollback posture.

Stays private: unverified evidence, internal review notes, customer data, and private governance material.

Operational handoff Make the next operator visible.

Assistant may draft: task packets, acceptance checks, status summaries, and route options.

Human approves: commits, provider action, public exposure, payments, and workflow authority.

Stays private: raw logs, credentials, local configs, private account data, and sensitive personal context.

What this is not

Not A Substitute For Human Judgment

VaporHuman is not a person, therapist, doctor, lawyer, accountant, employer, identity service, emergency service, or authority over human decisions.

It does not promise that AI is always right, always safe, private by default, always available, or a substitute for human judgment.

Why it exists

Keep the human visible.

AI work gets expensive when every session restarts from zero. VaporHuman goes the other way.

The goal is to make assistant systems more useful in practice: what they know, what they do not know, what they are allowed to do, what should stay private, and when a human needs to approve the next move.

Start with Work with VaporHuman, then read the Trust boundary.

Current posture

Early, modest, and bounded.

The current site is static. Work intake is hosted by Tally, paid discovery is hosted by Stripe, and the shop is hosted by Fourthwall. There is no embedded login, checkout, tracking script, hidden form, or live assistant service in this build.

Useful first. Honest always. Human in charge.

VaporHuman designs private AI continuity systems. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the product lane develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or regulated advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

The work-intake path uses a hosted Tally form, and paid discovery uses a hosted Stripe Payment Link. This site does not embed forms, login, checkout, public email address, public support flow, subscriptions, or live assistant service. This build does not include analytics or tracking scripts.
