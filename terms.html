Terms

Use VaporHuman with clear boundaries.

These terms describe the current public site, hosted intake, hosted paid discovery, and provider boundaries.

The site is live, but it is still staged: service scope, payments, support, analytics, accounts, and provider integrations expand only through explicit gates.

Current date

Effective May 21, 2026.

By using vaporhuman.com, submitting the hosted intake form, opening the Stripe paid-discovery link, or visiting the Fourthwall shop, you agree to use these workflows within the limits described on this page.

Active workflows

What is available now.

Work intake

The Tally-hosted intake form helps route qualified requests. Submitting a form does not guarantee acceptance, response time, or project availability.

Paid discovery

The Stripe-hosted paid discovery session is a remote review of your request and a recommendation for the next practical step.

Creator shop

The Fourthwall-hosted shop is separate from VaporHuman service delivery. Fourthwall handles shop checkout, fulfillment, and related shop workflows.

Non-claims

What VaporHuman does not promise.

No emergency use

Do not use VaporHuman for emergency, crisis, safety, medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, or regulated decisions.

No guaranteed outcome

Discovery, recommendations, site copy, assistant output, or workflow advice do not guarantee revenue, ranking, approval, compliance, or future project acceptance.

Human responsibility

You remain responsible for reviewing decisions, protecting private information, verifying facts, and deciding whether to use any recommendation.

Provider terms

Hosted providers have their own terms.

Intake provider

Tally

Tally hosts the intake form. Tally's terms are available at tally.so/terms.

Payment provider

Stripe

Stripe hosts the paid-discovery checkout. Stripe's service agreement is available at stripe.com/legal/ssa.

Shop provider

Fourthwall

Fourthwall hosts the shop. Fourthwall's terms are available at fourthwall.com/terms-of-service.

Paid discovery

Payment does not create unlimited service.

A paid discovery session covers a remote review and recommendation based on the information available. It does not include implementation, emergency support, regulated certification, tax/legal/financial advice, managed services, subscription access, or guaranteed acceptance into a larger project.

Larger work should be scoped separately after intake and discovery. Custom work may require a separate invoice, written scope, timeline, access plan, and support boundary.

Your responsibilities

Use the site and providers honestly.

You agree not to submit unlawful, abusive, misleading, infringing, or sensitive material through the public intake form. You also agree not to submit secrets, passwords, private keys, recovery codes, card numbers, bank details, identity documents, or emergency requests.

If you pay through Stripe or buy through Fourthwall, use accurate customer and payment information and review the provider-hosted checkout before completing payment.

Refunds and support

Support terms live on a separate policy page.

Refund, rescheduling, support, and provider-resolution posture is described at Refund and Support.

VaporHuman designs private AI continuity systems. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the product lane develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or regulated advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

The work-intake path uses a hosted Tally form, and paid discovery uses a hosted Stripe Payment Link. This site does not embed forms, login, checkout, public email address, public support flow, subscriptions, or live assistant service. This build does not include analytics or tracking scripts.
