Refund and support

Support starts with the hosted intake path.

VaporHuman has live intake and paid discovery, but no public email, account portal, subscription system, or live assistant support service yet.

This page explains how to route support questions, refund requests, shop issues, and provider problems while the service lane is still early.

Effective date Routing Refunds Live links No emergency support Request help

Current date

Effective May 21, 2026.

This policy covers VaporHuman paid discovery and the linked hosted provider paths. It does not replace the terms or support workflows of Tally, Stripe, Fourthwall, NameSilo, GitHub Pages, or any other provider.

Where to go

Route support to the right host.

Work intake

For work-intake, privacy, support, or scheduling questions, use the Tally-hosted intake form and include only the minimum context needed.

Paid discovery

Stripe hosts the paid-discovery payment page and receipts. VaporHuman handles discovery scheduling and scope review after payment.

Shop orders

Fourthwall hosts the shop. Merch, memberships, shipping, and shop-order issues should be handled through Fourthwall's shop support flow.

Paid discovery refunds

Refunds are reviewed against delivery state.

Before delivery

If a paid discovery session cannot reasonably be scheduled or delivered, request a refund or reschedule review before the session is completed.

After delivery

Completed discovery sessions are generally not refundable unless there is a clear duplicate payment, provider error, or delivery issue.

Custom work

Larger scoped work, invoices, subscriptions, deposits, retainers, and suite services require separate written terms before activation.

Live links

Use the active provider route.

Support intake

Open the intake form

Use this for work requests, scheduling context, support requests, or privacy requests until a public support email is established.

Paid discovery

Open Stripe payment link

Stripe handles hosted checkout and receipts for the paid discovery session.

Creator shop

Open Fourthwall shop

Fourthwall handles the creator shop, checkout, order support, and fulfillment-related workflows.

No emergency support

Do not use VaporHuman as urgent support.

VaporHuman does not provide emergency, safety, medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, payroll compliance, tax filing, or crisis support. Do not submit urgent requests through the intake form.

Payment, checkout, domain, DNS, provider, and technical issues may depend on third-party support queues and cannot be guaranteed to resolve within a specific timeframe.

How to request help

Include only enough detail to identify the issue.

For VaporHuman paid-discovery support, submit a request through the work-intake form. Include the same name or email used for payment and a short description of the issue. Do not submit full card numbers, bank details, passwords, recovery codes, or private keys.

For Fourthwall shop issues, use the shop's Fourthwall support path. For Stripe payment receipt issues, review the Stripe-hosted receipt or payment confirmation first, then contact VaporHuman with minimal identifying context.

VaporHuman designs private AI continuity systems. Site copy, page structure, availability, and future features may change as the product lane develops.

This site does not provide medical, legal, financial, employment, identity, emergency, or regulated advice. Assistant output should support human judgment, not replace it.

The work-intake path uses a hosted Tally form, and paid discovery uses a hosted Stripe Payment Link. This site does not embed forms, login, checkout, public email address, public support flow, subscriptions, or live assistant service. This build does not include analytics or tracking scripts.
